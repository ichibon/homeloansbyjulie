01 · Understanding Your Costs
Closing Costs vs. Prepaids
Same page at closing. Very different things.
Closing Costs
Fees to get the loan done
Appraisal, title, attorney, lender fees. Pay once, they're gone.
Prepaids
Your money, held for you
Insurance + tax funds. You'd pay these anyway — we collect them early.
💡 Key Insight
Third-party fees are the same with any lender. Shopping around only changes the lender's own fees.
2/10
02 · Understanding Your Costs
HOA Community Costs
Three fees that catch buyers off guard.
Initiation Fee
One-time welcome fee from the HOA. Varies by community.
Prorated Dues
Reimburse the seller for their prepaid dues. Calculated by the day.
Closing Letter Fee
Lenders require it. HOA charges $100–$300 to prepare it.

06 · Managing Your Mortgage
How to Get Rid of PMI
Mortgage insurance is temporary. Three ways out — whichever comes first.
1
Request removal at 20% equity
Call your servicer, order an appraisal. PMI removed if confirmed.
2
Auto-removal at 78% of purchase price
Happens automatically. No action needed on your part.
3
Recast within your first 24 payments
Make a lump-sum payment, lower your balance below the threshold.
Required minimum: 2 years
Even with equity, you can't remove it before 24 months.
